Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie.

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
